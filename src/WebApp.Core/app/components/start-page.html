Introduction tour

Scan a shoe

If you want more information about a shoe you can move your phone closely to the bottom of the shoe. After the shoe was succesfully scanned a new view is openen with detailed information about the shoe.

Click and collect a shoe

On the product view you'll find usefull information about a shoe like materials, price, video's and more. Also, the current store stock of the shoe is displayed. If your shoe isn't available anymore you can use our Click and Collect feature to order the shoe in a different store.

My orders

The my orders view contains an overview of all your open orders. Foreach order the expiration date is displayed, but you're also able to call the store directly, ask a question or show the direction to that store on a Google Map

Wishlist

Do you still have some doubts to buy a shoe. No problem, with our wishlist feature you can easily add that shoe to the wishlist. This view provides an overview of all shoes that have been added plus the store from where the shoe was added.
